<p>Are you an aspiring global e-commerce seller eager to tap into the vast potential of the US market? If so, this exclusive workshop, developed in collaboration with Amazon, is tailor-made just for you. Designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of selling on Amazon.com and navigating the intricacies of the US market, you will gain invaluable insights and practical skills necessary to help you succeed in your cross-border e-commerce journey.</p>

<p>Join us as we delve into the world of global selling on Amazon.com - from optimising product listings to understanding profit margins and ROI calculations, you will acquire essential strategies and techniques to effectively market your products to the millions of customers who choose Amazon for their shopping needs. The workshop encompasses a wide array of crucial topics, including legal and regulatory requirements for international selling, product sourcing, brand registry, trademarking, and fulfilment options. Furthermore, you will develop a deep understanding of the Amazon.com platform and its rules and policies, empowering you to make informed decisions and foster business growth with direct support of the Amazonâ€™s advertiser success team. Get ready to embark on an exciting path of digital entrepreneurship as you hone your selling strategies on Amazon.com!  YouTube!</p>


<h4>Course Title | Mode of Training | Course Ref</h4>

<p>Digital Entrepreneurship in the Era of Cross-Border E-Commerce: Mastering Selling Strategies on Amazon.com<br>Classroom &amp; Synchronous E-Learning - TGS-2023021046</p>

<h4>Outline</h4>
* Understand the US market trends and explore global selling on Amazon.com
* Identify the legal and regulatory requirements for selling internationally and ensure compliance
* Discover the critical steps involved in sourcing and selecting products, calculating ROI, and setting pricing successful selling on Amazon.com
* Develop a comprehensive global selling strategy that incorporates essential aspects such as market research, localisation, and international shopping trends
* Create an omnichannel plan to integrate Amazon.com with other sales channels, expanding your reach to a wider audience and driving business growth

<h4>Who Should Attend</h4>
<p>This workshop is suitable for Business Owners, Entrepreneurs, Marketing Executives/Managers, and individuals who are interested to generate side income through selling on Amazon.com</p>
	
<h4>Requirements</h4>
<ul>
<li>Bring your own laptop</li>
<li>Have prior experience in online shopping and/or operating e-commerce business
</li></ul>

<h4>Duration</h4>
<p>2 days (9am - 6pm)<br>Includes 8 hours Synchronous E-Learning</p>

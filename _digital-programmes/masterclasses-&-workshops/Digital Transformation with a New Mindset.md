<p>More often than not, digital transformation is not just about the technology but the people and their ability to harness the power of digital to support daily operations at work or business.</p>

<p>While the retail landscape continues to evolve along with new technologies providing efficiency, retail is still a people business which requires you to take informed action to understand, reach out and engage with your consumers the right way.</p>

<p>Cultivate a new mindset to thrive in this ever-changing digital-first environment. This exciting hands-on workshop will broaden your horizon on emerging technologies and trends that are reshaping the retail industry and allows you the opportunity to try your hands at creating AI-generated art, avatars and more!</p>

<h4>Course Title | Mode of Training | Course Ref</h4>

<p>Digital Transformation with a New Mindset
<br>Classroom - TGS-2022014050
</p>

<h4>Outline</h4>
* Develop a digital mindset to improve your agility for digital transformation
* Understand the six critical mindset dispositions for a successful digital transformation of your business
* Recognise the new customer shopping journey and leverage emerging trends to improve customer experience
* Adopt emerging technology and content to engage digitally savvy customers

<h4>Who Should Attend</h4>
<p>Marketing Professionals, Business Owners, Entrepreneurs, and individuals who create marketing copy.</p>
